Reflection # 1: My First Few Weeks of USERDES Class

My experiences in my first few weeks of USERDES class was energetic since our prof is trying to make our online class lively and we must cooperate and participate to make ourselves be knowledgeable in this class. The kahoot also makes our class participative and using kahoot test our capability if we already know some ideas about the topics relating about USERDES. So far, I have learned what is the definition and the concepts of Design/User Design, UI/UX, and we also applied the design in making a website and personas. This gives me an idea on how to utilize in making a design in the future since clients have different taste in wanting a design that they like.
